About Calaguas Island

Located in the province of Camarines Norte, Calaguas Island is known for its long stretch of powdery white sand beach called Mahabang Buhangin. Unlike more commercialized beaches, Calaguas remains relatively undeveloped, offering visitors a truly natural and peaceful beach experience.

Our travel agency has been organizing tours to Calaguas since 2010, with a focus on sustainable tourism that preserves the island's natural beauty while providing memorable experiences for our guests.
